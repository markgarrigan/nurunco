Tuesdays at the Creek

We'll meet at the lower Baird Creek parking lot west of the Hwy 43 overpass on Baird Creek Rd.

If you've never been to the creek just let us know and someone who has, will stay with you the whole time. We'll run three sections which are 4.3ish miles, 1.2ish miles, and 3.4ish miles respectively. Here's a map.

After August a headlamp is pretty much required.
